# Known Competitors — Paleo

## Direct competitors
- **Covermanager:** https://covermanager.com — Reservation software for restaurants. €49/month. Reservations only, no inventory or POS.
- **Agora POS:** https://agorapulse.com — POS for hospitality. €99/month. Cash register/payments only, no integrated inventory or reservations.
- **Revo:** https://revo.works — Management system for hospitality. Oriented toward mid-sized chains, pricing not public (enterprise).

## Indirect competitors or substitutes
- **TheFork:** https://thefork.es — Reservation marketplace with commission. Not a management SaaS.
- **Excel + WhatsApp:** The current solution used by most of our potential customers.
- **7Shifts:** https://7shifts.com — Shift management for restaurants. Focused on the US, no Spanish localization, no inventory.

- Category / vertical: Operational management SaaS for independent restaurants
