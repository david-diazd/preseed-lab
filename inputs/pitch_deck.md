# Paleo — Pitch Deck

## The problem

74% of independent restaurants in Spain close within 5 years. The main cause isn't the food — it's management. A mid-sized restaurant loses between €800 and €1,500 per month due to uncontrolled inventory shrinkage, order errors, and poorly optimized labor costs.

Independent restaurant managers run their business with Excel, WhatsApp, and intuition. They don't have the time or budget for the enterprise solutions used by large chains.

---

## The solution

**Paleo** is the management software designed specifically for independent restaurants.

A single system that integrates:
- **Smart inventory** — real-time stock control, stockout alerts, shrinkage reduction
- **Reservation management** — calendar, automatic confirmations, waitlist
- **Integrated POS** — cash register, payments, tips, automatic end-of-day closing
- **Cost analytics** — food cost per dish, margin by category, daily P&L
- **Automatic reports** — the manager receives a weekly summary every Monday

Price: **€149/month** per restaurant. No commissions, no surprises.

---

## Why now

Three structural changes open this window:

1. **Affordable cloud computing** — 5 years ago, integrating POS + inventory + reservations required expensive infrastructure. Today it costs €50/month on AWS.
2. **Post-COVID digitalization** — 68% of restaurants in Spain adopted some digital tool between 2020-2022. The market is ready for the next step.
3. **Margin pressure** — inflation from 2022-2024 compressed margins to the limit. Restaurant owners are actively looking for tools that reduce costs, not just manage them.
